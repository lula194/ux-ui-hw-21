Fly Assist

This is a case study I created to help users book trips and flights without hassle

I used Figma to create a mockup of the prototype for FlyAssist.



As a user researcher, I want to understand the pain points people have as they plan for traveling.

After conducting a few user interviews, I realized that users struggle with slow online service and have a hard time planning,

which makes users have an overall negative experience.



Problem Statement:FlyAssist was designed to achieve seamless travel booking for travelers.

We have observed that users were frustrated with slow customer service and the lack of affordable travel options

How might we operate FlyAssist so that our users are satisfied based on reviews and app activity?



Based on the user testing results, I went an added additions such as a page that lists out all of your planned excursions.

Another interation I implemented was linking the sign-in page with your existing email, for a smoother login process
