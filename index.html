I'm a UX/UI designer based in Maryland, USA

I design webpages and applications, with a passion for problem solving and critical thinking. I strive to seamlessly blend business and user needs.



My UX Skills

User Research

I plan and conduct research to gather insights for design decisions. These insights inform user-centric interfaces, with a continuous feedback for ongoing improvements. This approach results in visually appealing and intuitive designs that evolve to meet changing user needs over time.

Wireframing

I utilize wireframe prototyping to help bring my designs to life. Wireframes help visualize and communicate design concepts.These wireframes serve as a blueprint, ensuring a structured and user-focused layout. Through this approach, I've delivered successful user-friendly designs.

User Testing

User testing plays a pivotal role in ensuring the effectiveness of my designs. During the evaluation process, I gather valuable feedback that guides refinement and improvement. By conducting user testing, I'm able to verify the usability and functionality of digital interfaces, pinpointing any areas for enhancement.
